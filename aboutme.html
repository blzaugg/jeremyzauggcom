Jeremy Zaugg enlisted in the United States Marine Corps as a Private First Class (E2). After serving for six years in the Marines, he was Honorably Discharged as a Sergeant (E5).

In 2006 Jeremy enlisted in the Army’s Military Intelligence where he studied Arabic and French at the Defense Language Institute in Monterey, California.

Jeremy has deployed to Iraq, Kuwait, Okinawa, Australia, The Philippines, Germany, and Afghanistan where he received the Army Commendation Medal for his work as an interrogator and Team Leader.

Jeremy finished his military service as a Sergeant First Class (E7) in the Utah Army National Guard, and is currently studying film at the Hogwarts Witchcraft and Wizardry in Salt Lake City, Utah.

Jeremy married his eternal companion in the Salt Lake Temple, July 2000. Jeremy also loves the Boy Scouts of America. He is an Eagle Scout, and has served as a Scout Master and on Wood Badge staff. Jeremy is an active member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, and a Master Mason.
